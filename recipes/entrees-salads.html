Raw Zucchini "Spaghetti" with Marinara

Fresh spiralized zucchini topped with a rich, flavorful raw marinara sauce and fresh herbs.

20 minutes Serves 2-3 Raw • Vegan • Gluten-Free

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium zucchini, spiralized
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cup sun-dried tomatoes, soaked
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Pine nuts for garnish
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Spiralize zucchini and lightly salt, let drain for 10 minutes
  2. Blend cherry tomatoes, soaked sun-dried tomatoes, olive oil, and garlic
  3. Add basil, lemon juice, oregano, salt, and pepper to sauce
  4. Pat zucchini noodles dry with paper towels
  5. Toss noodles with marinara sauce
  6. Garnish with pine nuts and fresh basil
  7. Serve immediately

Health Benefits:

This dish is rich in lycopene from tomatoes, healthy fats from olive oil, and provides plenty of vitamins and minerals from fresh vegetables.

Raw Taco Bowl with Walnut "Meat"

A satisfying taco bowl featuring seasoned walnut "meat," fresh vegetables, and creamy avocado.

25 minutes Serves 3-4 Raw • Vegan • Gluten-Free

Ingredients for Walnut "Meat":

  • 2 cups raw walnuts
  • 2 tablespoons tamari or coconut aminos
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Bowl Ingredients:

  • 4 cups mixed greens
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 2 avocados, sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, diced
  • Lime wedges
  • Fresh cilantro

Instructions:

  1. Pulse walnuts in food processor until crumbly
  2. Add tamari and spices, pulse to combine
  3. Don't over-process - keep texture chunky
  4. Arrange greens in bowls
  5. Top with walnut "meat" and all vegetables
  6. Garnish with avocado slices and cilantro
  7. Serve with lime wedges

Health Benefits:

Walnuts provide omega-3 fatty acids and protein, while the colorful vegetables offer a wide range of vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.

Rainbow Power Salad

A vibrant, nutrient-dense salad featuring every color of the rainbow for maximum health benefits.

15 minutes Serves 2-3 Raw • Vegan • Gluten-Free

Salad Ingredients:

  • 4 cups mixed baby greens
  • 1 cup purple cabbage, shredded
  • 1 large carrot, julienned
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 cup hemp seeds
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds
  • Fresh herbs (parsley, cilantro, basil)

Lemon Tahini Dressing:

  • 3 tablespoons tahini
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon maple syrup
  • 2-3 tablespoons water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Arrange mixed greens as the base
  2. Add vegetables in colorful sections
  3. Sprinkle with hemp and pumpkin seeds
  4. Whisk all dressing ingredients together
  5. Add water gradually until desired consistency
  6. Drizzle dressing over salad
  7. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve

Health Benefits:

This colorful salad provides a wide spectrum of antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals. The tahini dressing adds healthy fats and protein for satisfaction.

Entree & Salad Success Tips

Color Variety

Include vegetables of different colors to ensure you're getting a wide range of nutrients and antioxidants.

Texture

Combine different textures - crunchy nuts, creamy avocado, and crisp vegetables for satisfying meals.

Tools

Invest in a good spiralizer, mandoline, and sharp knives to make raw food preparation easier and more enjoyable.

Storage

Prepare components ahead and store separately. Assemble just before serving to maintain freshness and texture.
