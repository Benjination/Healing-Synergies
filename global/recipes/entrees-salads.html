Entrees & Salads

Satisfying main dishes and fresh salad combinations that prove raw food can be hearty, flavorful, and completely satisfying.

Hearty and Nutritious Meals

These substantial raw entrees and vibrant salads are designed to nourish your body while satisfying your appetite with fresh, whole ingredients.

Raw Veggie Lasagna

Serves 2-4

  • 2 zucchini, thinly sliced lengthwise
  • 1 cup marinara sauce (see recipe below)
  • 1 cup cashew cheese (see recipe below)
  • 1 cup spinach leaves
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced

Layer zucchini slices, marinara, cashew cheese, spinach, mushrooms, and bell pepper in a dish. Repeat layers. Serve immediately or chill for 1 hour for best texture.

Cashew Cheese

  • 1 cup raw cashews, soaked 2 hours
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon nutritional yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 cup water

Blend all ingredients in a food processor until smooth and creamy.

Marinara Sauce

  • 2 cups tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, soaked
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil

Blend all ingredients in a blender until smooth.

Rainbow Raw Salad

  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1 carrot, shredded
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup sunflower seeds
  • 1/4 cup raisins

Toss all ingredients together in a large bowl. Serve with your favorite raw dressing.

Raw Taco Wraps

  • 1 cup walnuts
  • 1 tablespoon tamari
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Romaine lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomato, avocado, and sprouts

Pulse walnuts, tamari, and spices in a food processor until crumbly. Spoon mixture into lettuce leaves and top with tomato, avocado, and sprouts.

Simple Raw Cucumber Salad

  • 2 cucumbers, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped

Toss all ingredients together and let sit for 10 minutes before serving.

Entree & Salad Success Tips

Color Variety

Include vegetables of different colors to ensure you're getting a wide range of nutrients and antioxidants.

Texture

Combine different textures - crunchy nuts, creamy avocado, and crisp vegetables for satisfying meals.

Tools

Invest in a good spiralizer, mandoline, and sharp knives to make raw food preparation easier and more enjoyable.

Storage

Prepare components ahead and store separately. Assemble just before serving to maintain freshness and texture.
