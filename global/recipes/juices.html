Green Detox Juice

A powerful green juice blend with cucumber, celery, kale, and lemon for ultimate detoxification and energy.

10 minutes Serves 2 Raw • Vegan • Gluten-Free

Ingredients:

  • 2 large cucumbers
  • 4 celery stalks
  • 4 cups fresh kale leaves
  • 2 green apples
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1 inch fresh ginger
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves

Instructions:

  1. Wash all produce thoroughly
  2. Run cucumber and celery through juicer first
  3. Add kale leaves slowly through juicer
  4. Juice apples and ginger
  5. Add fresh herbs at the end
  6. Stir in fresh lemon juice
  7. Serve immediately over ice

Health Benefits:

High in chlorophyll for blood purification, vitamin C for immunity, and enzymes for digestion. Perfect for morning detox!

Red Vitality Juice

A vibrant red juice featuring beets, carrots, and apples for cardiovascular health and natural energy.

12 minutes Serves 2 Raw • Vegan • Gluten-Free

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium red beets
  • 4 large carrots
  • 2 red apples
  • 1 inch fresh ginger
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil

Instructions:

  1. Scrub beets and carrots clean
  2. Cut produce to fit juicer chute
  3. Juice beets first (stains less this way)
  4. Follow with carrots and apples
  5. Add ginger and basil
  6. Stir in fresh lemon juice
  7. Strain if desired and serve fresh

Health Benefits:

Beets support cardiovascular health and natural nitrates boost energy, while carrots provide beta-carotene for eye health.

Citrus Immune Boost

A zesty blend of oranges, grapefruits, and lemon with ginger and turmeric for immune system support.

25 minutes Serves 3-4 Raw • Vegan • Gluten-Free

Ingredients for Walnut "Meat":

  • 2 cups raw walnuts
  • 2 tablespoons tamari or coconut aminos
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Bowl Ingredients:

  • 4 cups mixed greens
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 2 avocados, sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, diced
  • 4 large oranges
  • 2 large grapefruits
  • 2 lemons
  • 1 inch fresh ginger
  • 1/2 inch fresh turmeric
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon raw honey (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Peel citrus fruits, leaving some white pith
  2. Juice oranges and grapefruits first
  3. Add lemons for extra vitamin C
  4. Juice fresh ginger and turmeric
  5. Add pinch of black pepper to enhance turmeric
  6. Stir in honey if using
  7. Serve immediately for best flavor

Health Benefits:

Loaded with vitamin C for immune support, anti-inflammatory compounds from turmeric and ginger, and natural enzymes.

Juicing Success Tips

Fresh is Best

Drink fresh juice immediately for maximum nutrient content and best flavor. Oxidation begins as soon as you juice.

Organic Choice

Choose organic produce when possible, especially for fruits and vegetables you're juicing with the peel on.

Balance

Balance sweet fruits with vegetables and add lemon or ginger to prevent blood sugar spikes and enhance flavor.

Clean Equipment

Clean your juicer immediately after use to prevent buildup and ensure your next juice tastes fresh and clean.

15 minutes Serves 2-3 Raw • Vegan • Gluten-Free

Salad Ingredients:

  • 4 cups mixed baby greens
  • 1 cup purple cabbage, shredded
  • 1 large carrot, julienned
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 cup hemp seeds
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds
  • Fresh herbs (parsley, cilantro, basil)

Lemon Tahini Dressing:

  • 3 tablespoons tahini
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon maple syrup
  • 2-3 tablespoons water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Arrange mixed greens as the base
  2. Add vegetables in colorful sections
  3. Sprinkle with hemp and pumpkin seeds
  4. Whisk all dressing ingredients together
  5. Add water gradually until desired consistency
  6. Drizzle dressing over salad
  7. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve

Health Benefits:

This colorful salad provides a wide spectrum of antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals. The tahini dressing adds healthy fats and protein for satisfaction.

Entree & Salad Success Tips

Color Variety

Include vegetables of different colors to ensure you're getting a wide range of nutrients and antioxidants.

Texture

Combine different textures - crunchy nuts, creamy avocado, and crisp vegetables for satisfying meals.

Tools

Invest in a good spiralizer, mandoline, and sharp knives to make raw food preparation easier and more enjoyable.

Storage

Prepare components ahead and store separately. Assemble just before serving to maintain freshness and texture.
