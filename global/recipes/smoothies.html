Green Power Smoothie

A nutrient-dense green smoothie packed with spinach, banana, and tropical fruits for sustained energy.

5 minutes Serves 2 Raw • Vegan

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 ripe banana, frozen
  • 1 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cucumber, peeled
  • 1 cup coconut water
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ginger
  • Juice of 1/2 lime

Instructions:

  1. Add coconut water and spinach to blender first
  2. Add frozen banana and pineapple chunks
  3. Add cucumber, chia seeds, ginger, and lime juice
  4. Blend on high for 60-90 seconds until smooth and creamy
  5. Pour into glasses and serve immediately
  6. Garnish with extra chia seeds if desired

Health Benefits:

This smoothie is rich in iron, vitamin C, potassium, and omega-3 fatty acids. The combination supports immune function, provides sustained energy, and aids in hydration.

Berry Antioxidant Smoothie

A vibrant berry smoothie loaded with antioxidants to support cellular health and provide natural sweetness.

5 minutes Serves 2 Raw • Vegan

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed berries (blueberries, strawberries, raspberries)
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 1 tablespoon ground flaxseed
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 handful of spinach (optional)
  • 1-2 dates, pitted (for extra sweetness)

Instructions:

  1. Place almond milk and spinach (if using) in blender
  2. Add berries, banana, and pitted dates
  3. Add almond butter, flaxseed, and vanilla
  4. Blend until smooth and creamy, about 60 seconds
  5. Adjust thickness with more almond milk if needed
  6. Serve in chilled glasses

Health Benefits:

Berries provide powerful antioxidants that fight inflammation and support brain health. The healthy fats from almond butter and flaxseed promote satiety and nutrient absorption.

Tropical Paradise Smoothie

Transport yourself to the tropics with this refreshing blend of mango, coconut, and citrus.

5 minutes Serves 2 Raw • Vegan

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen mango chunks
  • 1/2 cup pineapple chunks
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup coconut milk (full-fat)
  • 2 tablespoons shredded coconut
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • Juice of 1 orange
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Pinch of black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Combine coconut milk and orange juice in blender
  2. Add mango, pineapple, and banana
  3. Add coconut oil, shredded coconut, turmeric, and black pepper
  4. Blend until completely smooth and creamy
  5. Taste and adjust sweetness if needed
  6. Pour into glasses and garnish with coconut flakes

Health Benefits:

This tropical blend provides vitamin C, healthy fats, and anti-inflammatory compounds from turmeric. The combination supports immune function and provides sustained energy.

Smoothie Success Tips

Freezing

Use frozen fruits for thicker, creamier smoothies without needing ice that can water down the flavor.

Balance

Aim for a balance of fruits, greens, healthy fats, and protein for sustained energy and nutrition.

Greens

Start with mild greens like spinach and gradually work up to stronger flavors like kale or dandelion.

Timing

Drink smoothies immediately for best flavor and nutrient content, or store in fridge for up to 24 hours.
