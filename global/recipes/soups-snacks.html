Raw Gazpacho

A refreshing cold soup bursting with fresh tomatoes, vegetables, and herbs for a perfect summer meal.

15 minutes Serves 4 Raw • Vegan • Gluten-Free

Ingredients:

  • 6 large ripe tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 small red onion, minced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Avocado slices for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Roughly chop 4 tomatoes and blend until smooth
  2. Dice remaining tomatoes finely
  3. Combine diced vegetables in large bowl
  4. Add tomato puree and mix well
  5. Stir in olive oil and vinegar
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and fresh basil
  7. Chill for 2 hours and serve with avocado

Health Benefits:

Rich in lycopene and antioxidants from tomatoes, hydrating from cucumber, and loaded with vitamins C and K.

Power-Packed Trail Mix

A nutritious blend of nuts, seeds, and dried fruits for sustained energy and satisfaction on-the-go.

5 minutes Makes 4 cups Raw • Vegan • Gluten-Free

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup raw almonds
  • 1/2 cup walnuts
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin seeds
  • 1/4 cup sunflower seeds
  • 1/2 cup dried goji berries
  • 1/4 cup dried mulberries
  • 2 tablespoons cacao nibs
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt

Instructions:

  1. Combine all nuts and seeds in large bowl
  2. Add dried fruits and cacao nibs
  3. Sprinkle with sea salt
  4. Mix thoroughly until evenly distributed
  5. Store in airtight container for up to 2 weeks
  6. Portion into small bags for convenient snacking

Health Benefits:

Provides healthy fats, protein, and antioxidants for sustained energy. Perfect pre or post-workout fuel!

Raw Coconut Vegetable Soup

A warming, creamy soup made with young coconut meat and fresh vegetables for comfort without cooking.

25 minutes Serves 3-4 Raw • Vegan • Gluten-Free

Ingredients for Walnut "Meat":

  • 2 cups raw walnuts
  • 2 tablespoons tamari or coconut aminos
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Bowl Ingredients:

  • 4 cups mixed greens
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 cups young coconut meat
  • 1 1/2 cups coconut water
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup fresh corn kernels
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Blend coconut meat and coconut water until smooth
  2. Add lime juice and sea salt to coconut base
  3. Dice all vegetables into small, uniform pieces
  4. Warm soup gently by blending with warm water if desired
  5. Stir in diced vegetables
  6. Garnish with green onions and cilantro
  7. Serve in bowls with extra lime

Health Benefits:

Young coconut provides electrolytes and healthy fats, while fresh vegetables add vitamins and fiber for digestive health.

Rainbow Power Salad

A vibrant, nutrient-dense salad featuring every color of the rainbow for maximum health benefits.

15 minutes Serves 2-3 Raw • Vegan • Gluten-Free

Salad Ingredients:

  • 4 cups mixed baby greens
  • 1 cup purple cabbage, shredded
  • 1 large carrot, julienned
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 cup hemp seeds
  • 1/4 cup pumpkin seeds
  • Fresh herbs (parsley, cilantro, basil)

Lemon Tahini Dressing:

  • 3 tablespoons tahini
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon maple syrup
  • 2-3 tablespoons water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Arrange mixed greens as the base
  2. Add vegetables in colorful sections
  3. Sprinkle with hemp and pumpkin seeds
  4. Whisk all dressing ingredients together
  5. Add water gradually until desired consistency
  6. Drizzle dressing over salad
  7. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve

Health Benefits:

This colorful salad provides a wide spectrum of antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals. The tahini dressing adds healthy fats and protein for satisfaction.

Entree & Salad Success Tips

Color Variety

Include vegetables of different colors to ensure you're getting a wide range of nutrients and antioxidants.

Texture

Combine different textures - crunchy nuts, creamy avocado, and crisp vegetables for satisfying meals.

Tools

Invest in a good spiralizer, mandoline, and sharp knives to make raw food preparation easier and more enjoyable.

Storage

Prepare components ahead and store separately. Assemble just before serving to maintain freshness and texture.
