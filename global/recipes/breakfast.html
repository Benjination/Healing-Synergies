Breakfast Recipes

Start your day with wholesome raw breakfast options that provide sustained energy and essential nutrients to fuel your morning and beyond.

Energizing Morning Meals

These raw breakfast recipes are designed to kickstart your day with natural energy, fiber, and nutrients that will keep you satisfied and focused throughout the morning.

Raw Granola Bowl

A crunchy, satisfying granola made from nuts, seeds, and dried fruits - no baking required!

15 minutes Serves 4 Raw • Vegan • Gluten-Free

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed raw nuts (almonds, walnuts, pecans)
  • 1 cup mixed seeds (pumpkin, sunflower, chia)
  • 1/2 cup dried fruit (goji berries, raisins, dates)
  • 1/4 cup coconut flakes
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup or agave
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Pinch of sea salt
  • Fresh berries for serving
  • Almond milk for serving

Instructions:

  1. Roughly chop the nuts into bite-sized pieces
  2. Combine nuts, seeds, and coconut flakes in a large bowl
  3. Mix maple syrup, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt in a small bowl
  4. Pour wet ingredients over dry and mix thoroughly
  5. Add dried fruit and mix gently
  6. Serve immediately with fresh berries and almond milk
  7. Store remaining granola in an airtight container

Health Benefits:

This raw granola provides healthy fats, protein, and fiber to keep you satisfied. The nuts and seeds offer essential minerals like magnesium and zinc for optimal brain function.

Overnight Chia Pudding Parfait

A make-ahead breakfast that's creamy, nutritious, and topped with fresh fruits and nuts.

5 min prep + overnight Serves 2 Raw • Vegan

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon cacao powder (optional)
  • Fresh berries for layering
  • Sliced banana
  • Chopped almonds
  • Coconut flakes

Instructions:

  1. Whisk chia seeds, coconut milk, maple syrup, and vanilla
  2. Add cacao powder if making chocolate version
  3. Let sit for 5 minutes, then whisk again to prevent clumping
  4. Refrigerate overnight or at least 4 hours
  5. Layer pudding with berries, banana, and nuts in glasses
  6. Top with coconut flakes and serve chilled

Health Benefits:

Chia seeds are packed with omega-3 fatty acids, fiber, and protein. This pudding provides sustained energy and supports digestive health.

Raw Oat Porridge

A warming, comforting porridge made with soaked oats and topped with your favorite fruits and nuts.

10 min + 2 hrs soaking Serves 2 Raw • Vegan

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats (gluten-free)
  • 1 1/2 cups almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons ground flaxseed
  • 1 apple, grated
  • 2 tablespoons almond butter
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Fresh berries and nuts for topping

Instructions:

  1. Soak oats in almond milk for 2 hours or overnight
  2. Add ground flaxseed and let sit for 10 minutes
  3. Stir in grated apple, almond butter, and sweetener
  4. Add cinnamon, vanilla, and salt
  5. Mix well until creamy and combined
  6. Serve topped with fresh berries and chopped nuts
  7. Drizzle with extra almond milk if desired

Health Benefits:

This porridge provides complex carbohydrates, fiber, and healthy fats for sustained energy. The apple adds natural sweetness and additional fiber.

Raw Breakfast Success Tips

Prep Ahead

Many raw breakfast recipes can be prepared the night before, making mornings easier and stress-free.

Hydration

Soak nuts and seeds overnight to improve digestibility and nutrient absorption.

Seasonal

Use seasonal fruits for the best flavor and nutritional value. Frozen fruits work great too!

Balance

Include protein, healthy fats, and fiber in each breakfast for sustained energy throughout the morning.
