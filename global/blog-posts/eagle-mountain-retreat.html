Rejuvenating Retreat at Eagle Mountain Lake

Retreats Wellness

Enjoy the rejuvenating powers of silence, sound, reflection, movement, and nutrition in this overnight retreat at Eagle Mountain Lake, approximately 30 minutes north of downtown Fort Worth.

A Sacred Space for Healing

The retreat will be held in silence except for a sound healing session featuring the exquisite healing voice of opera soprano Anna Frederika Poppova, and the ethereal tones of crystal singing bowls, played by licensed massage therapist John Howell.

Nourishing Your Body

Delicious vegan meals will be served, with an emphasis on fresh, raw ingredients. Every meal is thoughtfully prepared to nourish not just your body, but your spirit as well.

Activities for Mind, Body & Spirit

The activities you may choose from include:

  • Massage - Deep relaxation and healing touch
  • Private gentle yoga - Connect with your inner peace
  • Simple, meditative qigong exercise - Cultivate life energy
  • Lounging in a hammock - Pure relaxation by the lake
  • Creating a vision board - Manifest your dreams
  • Stringing a bracelet - Mindful creativity
  • Journaling - Reflect and process your experience
  • Hiking at Eagle Mountain Park - Connect with nature (Sunday following the retreat)

Accommodation Options

Though the retreat is within commuting distance for those residing in Dallas/Fort Worth, women may choose to stay overnight in our women's bunk room. For men, as well as for women choosing to stay nearby, there are cabins at Eagle Mountain Park, as well as Airbnb and VRBO options nearby.

"The next retreat is planned for autumn, 2021. Please let me know if you are interested, and I will add you to a waiting list."

Join Us for Transformation

This retreat offers a unique opportunity to step away from the busy world and reconnect with your deepest self. In the peaceful setting of Eagle Mountain Lake, surrounded by nature's beauty, you'll find the space to heal, reflect, and rejuvenate.
